✅ What happened in March
March is centered on enabling Pangea's launch and launching Cube to support scalable, self-serve reporting.
  • Pangea prioritized across billing, eligibility, and campaign readiness Positions the business for a successful 4/1 launch and scalable campaign execution.
  • Cube reporting foundations significantly progressed — most Top P1 assets delivered, P2/P3 design underway Establishes the foundation for consistent, trusted self-service analytics.
  • QA automation expanded across tobacco surcharge alerts, Spectra QA, and ROI validation Reduces reporting and billing risk as delivery volume increases.
  • Member Strategy metrics refined to close key engagement and progression data gaps Enables better decisions on where interventions drive impact.
  • Clinical outcomes and KPI alignment advanced across reporting Strengthens consistency in measurement across Product, Clinical, and Growth.
  • High-volume operational support maintained — data feeds, claims, Iterable, Typeform, Sendoso, billing fixes
⏭️ What's on deck in April
April focuses on activating what's been built — ensuring new capabilities are usable, scalable, and tied to business outcomes.
  • Finalize Pangea go-live readiness across billing, eligibility, and marketing workflows
  • Begin Cube user onboarding and validation, enabling feedback and broader adoption
  • Re-architect Spectra into the Cube semantic layer to unify reporting and support scalable BI tooling
  • Advance product analytics (CMv3 + Amazon engagement) to better understand member behavior
  • Continue QA automation expansion to support scaling data usage
  • Begin eligibility and supplemental file refactor
◆ Notable changes in priorities
Shifting from balanced roadmap execution to Pangea-driven prioritization shaped much of how the sprint unfolded in March.
  • Shifted from balanced roadmap execution to Pangea-driven prioritization, with billing and eligibility work leading sprint sequencing
  • Increased focus on QA and data validation, driven by billing accuracy, campaign dependencies, and warehouse test failures
  • Elevated operational investigations and campaign support (Typeform, Iterable, Sendoso) to maintain continuity of live workflows
  • Introduced platform and data quality work (Spectra, Tonic, eligibility reconciliation) as prerequisites for scalable reporting and product readiness
