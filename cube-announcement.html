Meet Cube. Self-serve answers from Pelago data.

A faster, friendlier way for everyone at Pelago to ask questions of our data, without waiting on a SQL query or a dashboard request.

Watch the walkthrough
What is Cube?

Cube is our new semantic layer for Pelago data. It defines our metrics and dimensions in one consistent place, so when you ask a question, you get the same answer the rest of the company would get.

You can chat with Cube in plain English, build views without SQL, and trust that the numbers reflect how Pelago actually defines them.

One source of truth

Metrics like enrolled members, engagement, and ROI are defined once and used everywhere.

Ask in plain English

Skip the SQL. Type your question, get an answer, and explore the data behind it.

Built for everyone

Whether you're in Commercial, Clinical, Product, or Ops, Cube is designed for non-technical use.
